About Us

At Winged it, we're dedicated to providing exceptional makeup services that not only enhance our clients' natural beauty but also promote wellness and compassion. We're committed to using only non-toxic, cruelty-free products that align with our values of kindness and respect. Our goal is to create a safe, welcoming space where every client feels empowered and celebrated. We believe that every woman deserves to feel confident and beautiful, and we strive to bring out the best in each and every one of our clients. Whether it's a natural everyday look or a glamorous special occasion design, we're passionate about helping our clients achieve their vision. As we like to say, "I believe all women are pretty without makeup... but with the right makeup, can be pretty powerful."
